Health Center

Healthcare services for students and staff

University Health Center

The CDLU Health Center provides primary healthcare services to all students, faculty, and staff members. The center is equipped with basic medical facilities and staffed by qualified medical professionals.

Medical Staff

One medical officer (MBBS), two nurses, and support staff available during working hours.

Facilities

First aid, outpatient consultations, basic diagnostic tests, emergency care, and medicines at subsidized rates.

Emergency

24-hour emergency referral service. Ambulance available for transferring patients to civil hospital Sirsa.

Services Offered

  • OPD consultations (Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
  • First aid and emergency treatment
  • Annual health checkup for students
  • Eye checkup and dental camps
  • Health awareness programs
  • Referral services for specialized treatment
  • Vaccination camps

Location: Near Administrative Block, CDLU Campus

Emergency Contact: +91-1666-247048
