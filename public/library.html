Vivekananda Library

The intellectual hub of CDLU campus

About the Library

The Vivekananda Library at CDLU is a modern, well-stocked library that serves as the intellectual hub of the university. Named after the great spiritual leader Swami Vivekananda, the library supports teaching, learning, and research activities of the university community.

Collection

Over 50,000 books, 100+ journals, and a vast collection of e-resources covering all disciplines taught at the university.

Digital Resources

Access to e-journals, e-books, databases like INFLIBNET, N-List, Shodhganga, and National Digital Library.

Facilities

Wi-Fi enabled reading halls, digital section, reference section, and separate study areas for students and research scholars.

Library Timings

Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM | Sunday: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Services

  • Issue and Return of Books
  • Reference and Information Services
  • Inter-Library Loan Facility
  • Photocopying and Printing
  • Digital Library Access
  • Plagiarism Check Service
  • Orientation Programmes for New Users
