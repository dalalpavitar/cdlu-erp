CDLU Alumni Network

The CDLU Alumni Association serves as a bridge between the university and its former students. The association organizes networking events, reunions, and contributes to the university's development.

Alumni Registration

All former students of CDLU are encouraged to register with the alumni association to stay connected and contribute to the growth of their alma mater.

Notable Alumni

CDLU has produced distinguished alumni who have excelled in various fields including academics, civil services, law, business, and social work. The university takes pride in their achievements and contributions to society.
