Revaluation

Apply for revaluation of answer sheets

Revaluation Process

Students who wish to apply for revaluation of their answer sheets can apply online/offline as per university schedule. The revaluation is conducted as per university rules and norms.

Fee Structure

ParticularFee (Rs.)
Revaluation per paper500
Scrutiny per paper300
Photocopy of Answer Sheet200

How to Apply

  • Submit application form to the examination branch within 15 days of result declaration
  • Pay prescribed fee at the university cash counter or online
  • Attach self-attested copy of marksheet
  • Results of revaluation will be announced within 30 working days

Note: Revaluation is applicable only for theory papers. Practical examinations are not eligible for revaluation. The revaluation decision is final and binding.
