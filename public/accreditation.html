Accreditations & Recognitions

Our commitment to quality standards and excellence

Chaudhary Devi Lal University holds several prestigious accreditations and recognitions from national bodies, reflecting our commitment to maintaining high standards in higher education.

UGC 12(B)
NAAC Accredited
BCI Approved
AICTE Approved
AIU Member

UGC Recognition under Section 12(B)

The University Grants Commission (UGC) has recognized CDLU under Section 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956, making the university eligible for central assistance and development grants. This recognition is a testament to the university's adherence to UGC norms and standards.

NAAC Accreditation

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has accredited CDLU, evaluating the university on parameters including curriculum, teaching-learning, research, infrastructure, student support, and governance.

Bar Council of India (BCI) Approval

The law programmes offered by the university are approved by the Bar Council of India, ensuring that graduates are eligible to practice law in Indian courts.

All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) Approval

Technical and management programmes offered by the university are approved by AICTE, ensuring compliance with national technical education standards.

Association of Indian Universities (AIU) Membership

CDLU is a member of the Association of Indian Universities, facilitating academic collaboration and credit transfer with other member universities across India.
