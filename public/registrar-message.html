Registrar's Message

A message from the chief administrative officer

Dr. Registrar

Registrar, CDLU Sirsa

Dear Students and Visitors,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Chaudhary Devi Lal University, Sirsa. As the Registrar of this esteemed institution, I assure you of our commitment to transparent administration and student-centric services.

The university administration works tirelessly to ensure smooth academic and administrative operations. From admissions to examinations, from fee management to result declaration, every process is designed with efficiency and transparency in mind.

Our university has implemented a fully digital ERP system that streamlines various administrative processes, making it easier for students to access attendance records, fee details, timetables, examination results, and important notices from a single platform.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of governance and ensuring that all stakeholders - students, faculty, staff, and parents - receive timely and efficient services. The university follows all statutory regulations and guidelines prescribed by UGC and other regulatory bodies.

I encourage you to explore our website and take advantage of the various facilities and services offered by the university. If you have any queries or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our administrative office.

Dr. Registrar
Chaudhary Devi Lal University, Sirsa
