Examination Branch

The Examination Branch of CDLU is responsible for the conduct of all university examinations, including mid-term, end-term, practical, and viva-voce examinations. The branch also handles the preparation of results, issue of transcripts, and degree certificates.

The Controller of Examinations oversees the entire examination process, ensuring fair and transparent conduct as per university rules and UGC guidelines.
