Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Commitment to quality enhancement in higher education

About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at CDLU was established to develop and maintain quality benchmarks for the academic and administrative activities of the university. IQAC works towards continuous quality improvement and prepares the institution for NAAC accreditation.

Objectives

  • Developing quality benchmarks for academic and administrative activities
  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment
  • Arranging feedback from stakeholders
  • Organizing workshops and seminars on quality-related topics
  • Documenting and analyzing various programmes and activities

IQAC Committee

The IQAC functions under the Chairpersonship of the Vice-Chancellor with members including senior faculty, administrative officers, and external experts from academia and industry.
