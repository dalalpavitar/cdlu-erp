About the University

Chaudhary Devi Lal University (CDLU), Sirsa, is a premier state university established by the Government of Haryana under Act 9 of 2003. Named after Jan Nayak Chaudhary Devi Lal, former Deputy Prime Minister of India, the university is recognized by UGC under Section 12(B) and is spread across a sprawling 213-acre campus.

With 24 teaching departments, 62+ affiliated colleges, and 37+ academic programmes, CDLU is committed to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation.
