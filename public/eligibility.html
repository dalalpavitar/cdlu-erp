General Eligibility

The following eligibility criteria apply for admission to various programmes at Chaudhary Devi Lal University. Candidates must meet the minimum requirements as specified below.

Programme LevelMinimum QualificationMinimum MarksAge Limit
UG (BCA, BBA, B.Com, BA)10+2 in relevant stream45% (40% for reserved)No age limit
UG (B.Sc)10+2 with Science45% (40% for reserved)No age limit
UG (BA LLB)10+2 in any stream45% (40% for reserved)Max 20 years
UG (LLB)Graduation in any stream45% (40% for reserved)No age limit
UG (B.Ed)Graduation with relevant subjects50% (45% for reserved)No age limit
PG (MA, M.Sc, M.Com)Bachelor's degree in relevant discipline45% (40% for reserved)No age limit
PG (MCA)BCA or B.Sc with Mathematics50% (45% for reserved)No age limit
PG (MBA)Bachelor's degree in any stream50% (45% for reserved)No age limit
PhDMaster's degree in relevant discipline55% (50% for reserved)No age limit

Reservation Policy

Seats are reserved as per Government of Haryana norms: SC (20%), ST (7.5%), OBC (27%), EWS (10%), and PwD (3%). Relaxation in minimum marks applies as per government rules.
