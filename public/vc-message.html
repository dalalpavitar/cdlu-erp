Vice-Chancellor's Message

A word from the chief executive of the university

Prof. (Dr.) Vice-Chancellor

Vice-Chancellor, CDLU Sirsa

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Dear Students, Faculty, and Visitors,

Welcome to Chaudhary Devi Lal University, a centre of excellence in higher education and research. It is with great pride and enthusiasm that I welcome you to our vibrant academic community.

Since its establishment in 2003, CDLU has grown into a premier institution of higher learning in the region, offering a diverse range of academic programmes across multiple disciplines. Our university is committed to providing quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, research aptitude, and holistic development among students.

At CDLU, we believe in the transformative power of education. Our dedicated faculty members, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and student-centric approach create an environment where every student can thrive and realize their full potential. We are proud of our 213-acre lush green campus, modern laboratories, well-stocked library, and sports facilities that contribute to the overall development of our students.

The university is actively engaged in research and innovation, with several research projects funded by national agencies. We are connected to the National Knowledge Network via 1 GBPS optical fiber, providing seamless access to global knowledge resources.

As we move forward, our focus remains on academic excellence, research innovation, industry collaboration, and community engagement. We are committed to implementing the National Education Policy 2020 and preparing our students for the challenges of the 21st century.

I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the programmes, facilities, and opportunities that CDLU offers. Whether you are a prospective student, a researcher, or a visitor, you will find a welcoming and intellectually stimulating environment at CDLU.

Jai Hind!

Prof. (Dr.) Vice-Chancellor
Chaudhary Devi Lal University, Sirsa
